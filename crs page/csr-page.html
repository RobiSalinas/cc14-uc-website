Center for Social Responsibility

Goals

  1. To increase the community involvement and extension services of the university by reaching out to as many beneficiaries as possible with the private and government agencies;
  2. To conduct needs assessment and problem identification on communities for possible assistance, technical support and livelihood through programs/projects;
  3. To network with the local government units and non-government organizations for convergence of community development projects for a unified and integrated thrust in addressing needs and problems of identified communities or barangays;
  4. To involve stakeholders of the university in community extension services not only as part of their social responsibility but as a self-imposed duty; and To leave a sustainable program in the community by training leaders continually.

Mission

UC’s CSR Office aims to institutionalize an integrative community development program in empowering the less fortunate towards self-reliance in partnership with private and government agencies.

Vision

UC’s CSR Office envisions itself as a compassionate and responsive educational institution committed to serve the communities towards sustainable development and improvement of the quality of life of the less fortunate.
