Legal Aid Clinic



About

The College of Law of the University of the Cordilleras offers both Certification 1 and Certification 2 to students who will take the 2023 bar onwards. Only one certification is required to be compliant. But starting School Year 2022-2023, it will be offered as a stand-alone subject.

Mission

To develop through the CLEP a distinctive brand of legal advocacy that strengthens our adherence to justice, equality, and peace.

Vision

To serve the community especially the under-privileged by providing professional and efficient legal services through its Legal Aid Clinic.
