Center for Creative Productions

The University of the Cordilleras (UC) Center for Creative Productions (CCP) is the Performing Arts Center of the University that catalyzes performances either on-campus productions or official representations for off-campus events. The Center was established on January 8, 2008 unifying the different events. Since then, the Center has produced world-class performances garnering numerous awards in performing arts.
