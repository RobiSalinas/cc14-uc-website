Career Opportunities

Foreign Language Instructors

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Foreign Language and other related programs with specialization in Mandarin language and/or Spanish Language
  • Preferably with Master’s Degree in the field of specialization
  • Must have relevant trainings in field of specialization

Clinical Instructors

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing with license
  • Preferably with Master’s Degree in any related field of specialization
  • Must have experience in teaching as college instructor, relevant hospital and community work

School Nurse

  • Bachelor’s degree in Nursing and with license
  • Preferably with experience as ER Nurse
  • With training in basic occupational safety and health
