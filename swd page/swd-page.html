Student Welfare and Development

“Fostering learner’s diversity towards holistic development”

Information Service

Coordinates, prepares, validates, and disseminates relevant information to students through video presentations, zoom webinars, infographics, and other promotional materials which are posted on our FB Page and Canvas application.

Student-Discipline and Security

Provides judicious implementation of institutional rules and regulations governing student conduct behavior like procedures for complaints and grievances, absenteeism, and other situations/ that may compromise student safety and security.

Student Organization and Activities

Provides a systematic management and facilitation of student organizations and activities. Also, monitors, recognizes, and evaluates student organizations and activities.

Provides productive inputs in the development and enhancement of skills/ in leadership and interpersonal, and volunteerism to form and cultivate the champion spirit of a true UCian.

Scholarships

Provides education opportunities/ to the academically capable/ but financially strapped students/ through scholarship grants and special programs.

Coordinates with concerned government agencies/ in facilitating grants and scholarships to students/ through regular contact with the beneficiaries.

International Affairs and Services

Aids with the needs of foreign students and the management of International programs such as linkages and partnerships.

Monitors foreign students’ welfare and development by creating programs that cater to the socio-psycho-cultural, academic and non- academic needs of all foreign students.
